Likes

If you give me food, please consult this guide and this guide first for safe and unsafe foods, but when in doubt, mashed sweet potato without the skin is always a treat for me. Dislikes

Similarly, I don't do well with meeting other dogs on leash, but if you just keep my lead taut, I will calmly walk past other dogs on the sidewalk. I really don't like loud noises and I especially hate loud construction sounds, so please try to keep me somewhere quiet while we wait for my dad.
