If you're on this page, you've likely seen my professional profile on LinkedIn as well as my technical CV on Github. Here, I want to call out my creative work.

In my spare time, I moonlight as an artist and I do a lot of mixed media usually involving acrylic painting, watercolor, inking, and illustration. Additionally, after over a decade-long hiatus, I've found my love of fashion again and have gotten back into designing and creating custom womenswear. In this area, I'm currently working on my side project sequin.earth which explores the intersection of fashion and tech.

I have some longterm runway and photoshoot aspirations for my designs and I can't wait to show the world once the collections are more complete. For a snippet of past work, take a quick hop over to my portfolio on Instagram Werken Design or send me an email.
